Tran et al. BIB13960547 and Park et al. BIB17618504 created two datasets using images from Instagram, which is a photo-sharing social networking service. The dataset of Tran et al. has about 10K images, which are mostly from celebrities. However, Park et al. used their dataset for hashtag prediction and postgeneration tasks in social media networks. This dataset contains 1.1M posts on a wide range of topics and a long hashtag list from 6.3K users.
