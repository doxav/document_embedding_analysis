In this section, we provide an overview of text summarisation and Question Answering (QA) approaches targeted towards the medical domain. The goal here is to present some of the important research work in this area and analytically review more recent and promising approaches. The medical domain itself is quite broad, and so we attempt to adhere to research work that is relevant for EBM. It must be mentioned that, in the recent past, there has been steady ongoing research in biomedical and medical text processing BIB5375832  ]. However, compared to other domains, there is very little published research in summarisation and question answering BIB13787765 . A variety of tools and resources have also been made available to aid summarisation approaches in this domain.
