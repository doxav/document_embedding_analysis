Sentiment analysis refers to the management of sentiments, opinions, and subjective text. The demand of sentiment analysis is raised due to the requirement of analyzing and structuring hidden information, extracted from social media in form of unstructured data. The sentiment analysis is being implementing through deep learning techniques. Deep learning consists of numerous effective and popular models, these models are used to solve the variety of problems effectively. Different studies have been discussed in this review to provide a deep knowledge of the successful growing of deep learning applications in the field of sentiment analysis. Numerous problems have been resolved by having high accuracy of both fields of sentiment analysis and deep learning.
