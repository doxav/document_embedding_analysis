The first phase in the Full Coverage Algorithm is to parse a document into sentences BIB9766184 . During this phase, stop-words are removed and the Porter stemming algorithm is applied to stem the words in the document to their base forms. The entire document is then treated as a query to each individual sentence. The second step is to calculate the subset of sentences that cover the entire concept space of the document. The highest ranked sentence is selected using Cosine Similarity. The words that appear in the highest ranked sentence are removed from the query and the process is repeated until no words can be removed from the query, thus obtaining the summarized document. Mallett et al. also compared the Full-Coverage summarizer with MEAD and found that the Full-Coverage summarizer outperforms the MEAD clustering technique BIB9766184 .
