Recent advances in artificial neural technology, especially increasingly deep learning, have impacted heavily on the field of machine learning, often defining the state of the art in the solutions to a variety of complex problems in numerous disparate domains. Natural language processing is no exception. In many areas of NLP, the use of deep learning has produced results that have easily surpassed those obtained by other machine learning and statistical methods used for many years prior. In this section, introduction is provided to some of the most significant issues in NLP that draw attention of researchers and practitioners, followed by a brisk explanation of the various artificial neural architectures that have been used in NLP.
