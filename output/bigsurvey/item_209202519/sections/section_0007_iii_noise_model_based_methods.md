One way to overcome noisy labels is to explicitly or implicitly model the noise structure. Easiest option is to take noise as random and symmetric, but this is not usually the case. Noise is mostly dependent on various factors (e.g. y-dependent, xydependent) and is asymmetric. Therefore taking noise behavior into account can improve the performance significantly BIB6054025 . Methods in this section aim to model the noise and use this information during training for better performance. Following sections describe different approaches under this category.
