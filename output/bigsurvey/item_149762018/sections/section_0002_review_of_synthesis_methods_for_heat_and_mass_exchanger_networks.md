Application of heat exchanger networks (HENS) and mass exchanger networks (MENS) in CO 2 capture is an important strategy to minimize energy and utility targets of the capture process. Methodologies for the synthesis of HENS and MENS are broadly classified into two: (i) sequential and (ii) simultaneous synthesis methods. Section 2.1 briefly describes the sequential synthesis technique, while Sect. 2.2 discusses simultaneous synthesis technique.
