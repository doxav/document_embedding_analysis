Stanford Sentiment Treebank BIB990233 is the semantic lexical dataset annotated by Stanford University, including a finegrained emotional label of 215154 phrases in a parse tree of 11855 sentences. It is divided into two tasks, one is a twocategory task, including 6920 training sets, 872 validation sets, and 1821 test sets; one is a five-category task, which contains 11855 sentences and 215154 phrases (category 5).
