Fine-grained sentiment analysis is based on aspect-level, also known as feature-based opinion mining. At present, the network text is becoming more and more diversified. While finegrained sentiment analysis can extract the comment objects, object attributes and their corresponding emotional words in the comment at the data feature level, which can better meet the needs of people's sentiment analysis. But fine-grained analysis is still difficult for short texts, which have less information and are more difficult to extract semantic relations between words. Therefore, aspect-level sentiment analysis that centered on short texts of semantic richness is considered to be one of the most promising research directions in the future.
