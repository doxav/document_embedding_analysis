The F-BLS can replaces the feature nodes of BLS with a group of Takagi Sugeno fuzzy sub-systems, and the input data are processed by each of them BIB52006776 . Instead of aggregating the outputs of fuzzy rules produced by every fuzzy subsystem into one value immediately, all of them are sent to the enhancement layer for further non-linear transformation to preserve the character of inputs. The defuzzification outputs of all fuzzy subsystems and the outputs of the enhancement layer are combined to obtain the model output. The parameters of F-BLS consist of the weights connecting the outputs of the enhancement layer to the final output layer and the coefficients in the following part of fuzzy rules in every fuzzy subsystem, which can be calculated by pseudoinverse rapidly. Therefore, F-BLS can still retain the fast computational nature of BLS.
