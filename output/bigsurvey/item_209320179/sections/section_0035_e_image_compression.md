Image compression plays a crucial role in the transmission and storage. To reduce storage requires considerable memory, and degrade transmission requires high bandwidths, previous methods mainly focus on compressive sensing, which is a technology-based on sparse coding. Recently, image compression systems based on deep CNN architecture have become an active area of research. For example, ADMM-Net with convolution and non-linear transform can achieve high reconstruction accuracy for compressive sensing magnetic resonance imaging, and keeping the computational efficiency of the ADMM algorithm  . Based on deep CNN, a novel CNN architecture can be designed for semantic perceptual image compression, which can generate a map that highlights semantically-salient regions so that they can encode at a higher quality as compared to background regions.
