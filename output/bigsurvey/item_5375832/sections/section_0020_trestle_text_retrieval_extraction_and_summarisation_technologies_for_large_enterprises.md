Their system is in essence an Information Extraction system, which relies heavily on Named Entity (NE) recognition. For this system, also drug names and diseases are named entities, apart from the classical ones, such as organization, person and location. TRESTLE allows users to navigate through the Scrip articles, and thus find the information that they are interested in, using the named entities that the system has extracted, which are links to the original articles from which the NEs have been extracted. Apart from this, TRESTLE also creates single sentence summaries for each newsletter from the template that was filled by the Information Extraction process. A link is also provided to the original newsletter.
