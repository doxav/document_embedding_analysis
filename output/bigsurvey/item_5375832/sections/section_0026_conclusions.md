This survey presented the potential of summarization technology in the medical domain, based on the examination of the state of the art, as well as of existing medical document types and summarization applications. The challenges that the summarization research has to overcome need to be viewed under the prism of the requirements of the specific field. The scaling to large collections of documents in various languages and from different media, the generation of informative summaries using more sophisticated language and knowledge engineering techniques, the generation of personalized summaries, the portability to new subdomains, the design of evaluation scenarios which model real-world situations, the integration of summarization technology in practical applications such as the clinical workflow, are among the issues that the summarization community needs to focus on.
