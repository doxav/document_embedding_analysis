ORDER
On February 1, 2006, the Court denied Vernon Evans Jr.’s motion for a temporary restraining order and motion for preliminary injunction. Mr. Evans has filed a notice of appeal, and this Court has denied his motion for injunction pending the appeal.
If and when the State of Maryland executes Mr. Evans, it must file with the Court and provide Evans’s counsel with the following documents:
(i) The completed Lethal Injection Checklist for Evans; (ii) The EKG report for Evans; and (iii) Any post-mortem reports for Evans, such as the post-mortem blood
toxicology report prepared by the Office of the Chief Medical Examiner. The State may redact these documents in conformity with the practices in this case so far, e.g., names and security details.
