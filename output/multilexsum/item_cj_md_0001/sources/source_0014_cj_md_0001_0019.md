MEMORANDUM RE:

Evans v. Saar, Civil No. L-06-149

Dear Members of the Press:

The Court has received inquiries as to whether members of the press and the public will have access to the testimony in this case.

The courtroom will be open to the public on Tuesday, September 19th and Friday, September 22nd. It will be sealed on Wednesday, September 20th until approximately 3:30 p.m. and Thursday, September 21st until approximately the middle of the day. During those times, a
live feed of the proceedings will be available in Courtroom 7B.

Trial will resume the week of October 10th. The Court will post a schedule for that week’s live feed at that time.

Despite the informal nature of this memorandum, it shall constitute an Order of the Court and the Clerk is directed to docket it accordingly.

Very truly yours,

/s/ Benson Everett Legg
