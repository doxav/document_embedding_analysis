MEMORANDUM TO COUNSEL RE:

Evans v. Saar, et al., Civil No. L-06-149

Dear Counsel:

The parties have filed a joint motion requesting that the case be stayed until legislation now pending in the Maryland General Assembly has been resolved. The motion is hereby GRANTED. The parties shall file a status report every 90 days.

Despite the informal nature of this memorandum, it shall constitute an Order of the Court and the Clerk is directed to docket it accordingly.

Sincerely, /s/ Benson Everett Legg
